5. Why There Is No Park Gear

Manual transmissions do not have a dedicated Park position like automatics.

The car is held by the parking brake and, as backup, by leaving it in 1st gear or reverse depending on slope.

Never rely on gear alone. Always use the parking brake.

How It Works

  1. You move the shifter.
  2. The linkage moves the shift fork.
  3. The fork slides the locking collar.
  4. The synchronizer ring matches speeds.
  5. The collar locks the gear to the shaft.
  6. Power is delivered to the output shaft.
